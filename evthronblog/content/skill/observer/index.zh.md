### Max Tegmark - Our Mathematical Universe - My Quest for the Ultimate Nature"
- "There are 4 levels of parallel universes!" sounds absolutely pseudoscientific to me at first, but All the arguments from the book are very scientific and reasonable. They all stem from solid observations, then the author applies the philosophical stance of an ideal physicist and push them to the logical extreme that lead us to seeming absurd conclusions. This makes me realise, to protect the meaning of our world, I have to take a more aggressive philosophical stance on science.
